    DOC_UPLOAD -->|pdf-parse| EXTRACT[Raw Text & Page Count Extracted]

    subgraph WF_STEPS [Mastra Workflow Steps]
        direction TB

        STEP1 -->|documentProcessingAgent<br/>Gemini 2.5 Flash LLM| C_PARSE[Parse Clauses & Categories]
        C_PARSE -->|dbService| CLAUSE_DB[(MongoDB<br/>clauses collection)]
        C_PARSE -->|getEmbedding| EMBED_GEN[Gemini Embeddings]

        STEP1 --> STEP2[Step 2: generate-timeline-step]
        STEP2 -->|timelineAgent<br/>Gemini 2.5 Flash LLM| T_PARSE[Extract Obligations & Deadlines]

    DOC_UPDATE --> DOC_DB

%% =====================================================
%% SEMANTIC QA PIPELINE (ACTIVE FLOW)
%% =====================================================
subgraph QA_PIPE [Grounded Q&A Pipeline - Active]
    Q_IN[Lawyer Questions Matter] -->|POST /api/v1/matters/:matterId/qa| QA_ROUTE[QA Endpoint]
    QA_ROUTE -->|getEmbedding| Q_EMBED[Query Embeddings]
    Q_EMBED -->|qdrantService.searchPoints| QD_SEARCH{Qdrant Match}
    QD_COLL -.->|org_id filtered search| QD_SEARCH
    
    QD_SEARCH -->|Top 5 Matching Clauses| CONTEXT[Context Evidence Assembled]
    CONTEXT -->|qaAgent<br/>Gemini 2.5 Flash LLM| QA_GEN[Generate Grounded Answer]
    
    QA_GEN -->|Answer & Context| ENKRYPT[Enkrypt AI Guardrails Gate]
    ENKRYPT -->|evaluate: toxicity, pii, bias, injection| ENKRYPT_RES{Safety Check}
    
    ENKRYPT_RES -->|Trust Score & Safety Metadata| OUT[Grounded Answer + Sources + Trust Score]
end
